OUR AIMS

PUPILS AND FAMILIES FIRST

As a Trust we will deliver a school experience for all our pupils which increases the opportunities for them to reach their full potential. We will do this by delivering outstanding pedagogic practice, underpinned by research.

SKILLS & LEARNING FOR LIFE

Our learning opportunities will target the economic and social needs of the areas in which we work. We will seek to maximise everyones potential, providing the skills, knowledge and expertise needed to lead a successful and fulfilled life.

SIGNIFICANT INFLUENCE

The Trust will be a leader in the creation and dissemination of knowledge about what works in schools. We aim to be a MAT that creates opportunities for our communities making a difference locally, regionally and nationally.
